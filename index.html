Rules: You must play these mini tic-tac-toe games in Human English Reading Order: starting with the top left -- from left to right then down to the next row, left-most square. (If you're reading this intro, you know how to read left to right, congrats!) You can ONLY play one mini square game at a time and can only move on once the square has been won or tied. Best out of 9 mini-games wins! May the odds be ever in your favor!
